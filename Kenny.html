Kenny Johnston

Drywall Specialist | Bremerton, WA

About Kenny

Kenny Johnston is a 56-year-old man living in the heart of Bremerton, Washington. He's a no-nonsense kind of guy who knows his way around a tool belt. Right now, he’s putting in long hours working on a small house in East Bremerton, hanging drywall and turning chaos into craftsmanship.

The Project

The house he's working on may be small, but it’s got big potential. Kenny is patching, mudding, sanding — the whole nine yards. He takes pride in making things smooth and solid, one sheet of drywall at a time.

The Neighbor

Now, let’s talk about Ron.

Ron lives next door. Ron is... well, let’s just say he’s a character. He’s convinced the cops are watching him — drones, unmarked cars, "weird energy in the power lines," you name it. Most days, Ron peeks through his blinds like he's in a spy movie. Kenny mostly minds his own business, but every so often he ends up in a wild conversation that starts with “You didn’t hear it from me, but…”

Kenny's Philosophy

Kenny doesn’t sweat the small stuff. He likes his coffee like he likes his men: black and hot as hell.

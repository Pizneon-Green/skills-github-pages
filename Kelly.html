About Kelly

Forged in the golden light and laid-back vibes of Northern California, Kelly is a one-of-a-kind original. She's a person who brings sunshine with her, even to the famously overcast (but beautiful!) Pacific Northwest.

Recently, she answered the call of the salty air and evergreen horizons, making a new home in the charming maritime town of Keyport, Washington. Whether she's exploring the Puget Sound or just enjoying the quiet life, she's brought her unique blend of warmth and wit to the Kitsap Peninsula.

She's known for her kindness, her adventurous spirit, and a laugh that can be heard from across the marina.

From NorCal to the PNW

It's a tale of two coasts. From the rolling hills and sprawling vineyards of Northern California to the misty mornings and majestic mountains of Washington, Kelly's journey is one of beautiful contrasts. She's traded year-round sun for dramatic seasons, but has found beauty in both.

  • Then: In-N-Out Burger, Highway 1 drives, and warm summer nights.
  • Now: Ivar's Fish and Chips, ferry rides, and cozy coffee shops.

One thing remains constant: Kelly's awesome personality, which shines brightly no matter the weather report.
